Beautiful Art, Deep Roots About Our Coop

From modest beginnings, the shop has grown into a must-see destination for Islanders and visitors alike. Twenty-one members now exhibit a wide-ranging array of unique crafts, from fiber goods to pottery, fused and stained glass art, jewelry in a variety of styles, turned wooden household items, watercolor and acrylic paintings, candles, garden ornaments and many other made-in-Vermont items. You'll find gift ideas for showers and weddings, birthdays and both adult and children's events. It's a great place to do early Christmas shopping.
